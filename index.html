Before you begin

Maximize screen brightness
Low brightness shifts perceived hue and affects accuracy significantly.
Normal indoor or diffuse light only
Direct sunlight on the screen or strong ambient color will skew results.
Remove tinted lenses or glasses
Any color filter will compromise the test. Remove before starting.
This tool is for screening purposes only and does not constitute a medical diagnosis. For clinical color vision evaluation, consult a licensed optometrist or ophthalmologist. Accuracy depends on display calibration and ambient lighting conditions.
