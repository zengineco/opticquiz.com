Before you begin

Maximize screen brightness
Low brightness shifts perceived hue and measurably reduces plate test accuracy.
Normal indoor or diffuse light
Direct sunlight on screen or strong colored ambient light will skew results.
Remove tinted glasses or lenses
Prescription lenses are fine. Tinted, photochromic, or colored lenses are not.

Results Disclaimer
These are probabilistic estimates only — not diagnoses. Results are generated by a statistical model running in your browser using population-level priors. Screen calibration, ambient lighting, and response patterns all affect output. Do not use these results for any medical, employment, or safety decision. If you have concerns about your color vision or eye health, consult a licensed optometrist or ophthalmologist. Results are processed locally and never transmitted.
